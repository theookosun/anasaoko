Conclusion and References

Conclusion

The case for gender equality has been well documented in academic reports and blog posts. It is an issue that negatively affects the life of women, men and countries. The authors of the OECD report argue the importance of holistically addressing the root cause of gender inequality. It is important to note that political representation and societal expectations of women do not always align. To effectively address this, government officials must prioritize the elimination of normative gender expectations that minimize equality in daily life.
